The Venue

Jõekääru

Located north of Toronto, Jõekääru was the summer camp Liisbet went to as a camper and counsellor. One fateful summer, they went to visit Seedrioru, their arch-rivals, and Liisbet struck up a friendship with her counterpart, a young man named Tom. The rest, as they say, is history.

We are incredibly fortunate to celebrate our wedding ceremony and reception at Jõekääru, surrounded by nature and fond memories. Note that the ceremony will be taking place about 375m from the reception area, crossing 2 narrow footbridges. Please let us know down below if you have any mobility concerns, and we will get back to you with a solution.

Getting There From Toronto, take Hwy 404 north, then head east on Ravenshoe Rd. Note that GPS systems may not direct you properly for this last portion: turn north on Manniku Rd. just before the "Udora Ontario" sign, then turn right on Viru. At the end of Viru you'll turn left, and arrive! Alternatively, input "Estonian Children's Camp" rather than "38 Viru Ave." into your Maps software of choice and that should lead to the correct location.
